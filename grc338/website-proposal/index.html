Introduction

Frost Bites Ice Cream

Frost Bites Ice Cream makes plant-based ice cream that's creamy and delicious, so customers can enjoy nostalgic ice cream flavors guilt-free! The products are also organic and GMO-free, with ethically sourced incgredients.

Target audience

The target audience of this shop is people who enjoy eating sweet treats but can't eat traditional dairy ice cream, specifically those who are vegan, lactose intolerant, or allergic to dairy.

The primary goals of this audience is to enjoy ice cream and other frozen desserts without having to consume dairy products in the process. Many of them avoid dairy for medical or ethical reasons, and don't want to compromise that in order to enoy sweets.

About

Frost Bites is a family-owned and operated business that believes that everyone should be able to enjoy ice cream! Our ice creams are organic, GMO-free, and plant-based, meaning they're packed with antioxidants, healthy fats, protein, and more. Every one of our ingredients is sourced sustainably, so you can enjoy dessert without guilt.
