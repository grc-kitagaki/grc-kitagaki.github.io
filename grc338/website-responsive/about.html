About

Frost Bites is a family-owned and operated business that believes that everyone should be able to enjoy ice cream! Our ice creams are organic, GMO-free, and plant-based, meaning they're packed with antioxidants, healthy fats, protein, and more. Every one of our ingredients is sourced sustainably, so you can enjoy dessert without guilt.

All of our ingredients are grown in pesticide- and synthetic- free soil, and processed without artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors. The ingredients are non-GMO certified, and our manufacturing partners are, too.

The coconuts we use to make our ice cream are organically grown without any added water, and afte rharvesting, the husk is sent to be used as a sustainable biomass fuel. Any excess waste is composted and turned into natural fertililzer.
